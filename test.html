THE EXPERIENCE
A member of the team introduce the concept.
The user choose a food and he scans it.
The user insert the food in the right module thanks to light feedbacks.
a member of the team makes the user imagine that time is passing. A food is expiring: an alert appears on the calendar and the module became red.
The user take the food off the module. Then he starts to follow the suggested recipe.
At the end the team shows the AR app to explain the difference composition of modules.
